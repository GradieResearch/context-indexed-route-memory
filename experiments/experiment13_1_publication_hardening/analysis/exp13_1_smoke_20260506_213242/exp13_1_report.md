## Safety Checks Baked Into The Implementation

- The mode-slot table has `modes + 1` slots. When context/mode binding is disabled, the unbound mode uses the extra slot, preventing the historical out-of-bounds bug.
- Corruption diagnostics pass the corrupted `QueryContext` into both composition and route-table evaluation.
- Recurrence-disabled-at-evaluation and recurrence-disabled-throughout are represented as separate variants.
- Lesions include targeted route-critical edges and matched random controls.
- Every generated run is written under `analysis/<run_id>/`; the optional SQLite record is written under `runs/<run_id>.sqlite3`.

## Interpretation Guide

Expected patterns only: if the route field is mechanistically meaningful, route-table accuracy may remain locally high when recurrence is disabled, while multi-step composition should fall. Targeted lesions should impair composed traversal more than matched random lesions when the learned structure is route-critical.

## Caveats

- These artifacts are internal ablations and controls, not external baselines.
- Route-table accuracy is local transition knowledge and must not be treated as composed traversal.
- Small problem sizes can saturate; use `standard` or `full` for manuscript-facing analysis.
- Random seeds can produce unstable deltas; interpret mean effects with dispersion.
- Figures are summaries; raw CSVs remain the evidence source.
- This report should not be read as proof or as a causal claim without the validation report.
